Avocado Toast


Description

This truly is the best avocado toast you'll ever have. If you like an added protein boost, top with scrambled or boiled eggs.

Prep Time

5 minutes

Total time

5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 ripe avocado, smashed
  • 1/3 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 tablespoon sunflower nuts and/or roasted pumpkin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey
  • 1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Assemble the avocado toast by smashing and smearing the avocado onto the toast.
  2. Top with cottage cheese, then sprinkle with nuts/seeds, crushed red pepper flakes, salt and pepper to taste, and a drizzle of honey. Serve immediately.
